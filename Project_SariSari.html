General Description

Sari-Sari Slay: Sari-Sari Store Management System (SSMS) is a desktop application designed to support local sari-sari store owners in managing their day-to-day business operations efficiently. Many small business owners still rely on manual recordkeeping, which can lead to issues such as inaccurate inventory tracking, pricing errors, and difficulty in monitoring debts or sales. This project aims to automate those processes and provide a platform where strore owners can record transactions, monitor stock levels, and track overall sales performance.

Features include a Point-of-Sale System, Sales Tracker, Stock Tracker, and Debt Lists Tracker, the system provides a comprehensive solution for store management. By this, it ensures an improvement in efficiency in business performance through accurate calculations, and make data-driven decisions to sustain and grow their community-based businesses.

Objectives

Provide smooth and fast transactions with the customers and providing pricing information to avoid knowledge gaps.

Allow the owner to adjust product stock numbers.

Allow record and display the customers' purchases as history of sales for better monitoring

Implement debt monitoring to record customers' debts.

Target Users

Primary Users: Sari-Sari Store Owners, co-owners, and staff

Secondary Users: Online Sellers, Entrepeneurship students
