General Description

PreView is a movie ticketing website designed to enhance the decision-making experience of moviegoers by providing all the essential information they need before purchasing tickets. Many viewers often buy tickets without knowing sufficient details about a film they are about to watch, which can lead to disappointment and wasted time and expenses. PreView addresses this by providing users with complete and reliable movie information, including trailers, authentic reviews, and ratings from other users. Aside from movie ticket booking, PreView promotes a more interactive and confident movie-watching experience. The platform encourages user interaction through its community-based review system, making it both informative and engaging.

Objectives

Develop user-friendly movie ticketing platform.

Allow users to post and share movie reviews.

Provide interactive features for enhanced engagement.

Enhance overall customer satisfaction.

Help users make well-informed movie choices.
