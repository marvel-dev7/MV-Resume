Enova: Mobile Retail Application

Type: Internship — Mobile App UI Design

Year: 2024

Role/Contributions: Main UI Designer

Tool used: Figma

General Description

The Enova Mobile Retail App is a modern e-commerce platform conceptualized to enhance online shopping experiences through improved accessibility, interaction, and usability. It addresses several common issues found in existing retail applications, such as cluttered interfaces, limited accessibility features, inaccurate delivery tracking, and insufficient customer support.

The app’s name "Enova" is derived from the words "innovation" and "nova," This signifies a breakthrough or new beginning in the world of retail, highlighting the app’s goal to evolve and innovate the shopping experience. Its design integrates advanced interaction features like voice and visual search, real-time delivery tracking, and an intuitive interface to make shopping smoother and more efficient for all users.

Objectives

To improve user experience in online shopping through accessible and intuitive UI design.

To integrate interactive features that enhance user engagement and trust.

To create a clean and minimalist layout that promotes clarity and ease of navigation.

To apply HCI principles in solving common e-commerce usability issues.

Target Users

Tech-savvy consumers: who value convenience and modern features.

Busy individuals: who prefer the efficiency of online shopping.

Deal hunters: who look for discounts and promotions; and who prioritize price over brand loyalty.
