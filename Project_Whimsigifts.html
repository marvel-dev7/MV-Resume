Whimsigifts

Type: Academic Project — Website UI Design

Subject Course: E-Commerce

Year: 2023

Role/Contributions: Main UI Designer

Tool used: Canva

General Description

WhimsiGifts is an e-commerce website concept designed to make gift selection easier and more personalized for users. The platform allows customers to choose from a variety of themed, pre-made gift baskets or create their own customized sets based on personal preferences. The goal was to design an interface that enhances the gifting experience through simplicity, creativity, and emotional connection.

This project was made under the E-Commerce course, focusing on the integration of usability and digital shopping convenience. The design emphasizes visual appeal, smooth navigation, and accessibility to help customers find thoughtful gifts quickly and efficiently. It also aims to promote local products and encourage meaningful gifting habits through a user-friendly online marketplace.

Objectives

To design an intuitive and visually engaging e-commerce interface.

To encourage personalized gifting and support local products.

To ensure a convenient and satisfying user experience for customers.

Target Users

Individuals looking for ready-made or personalized gifts.

Online shoppers seeking convenient gifting options.

Small business owners or local artisans selling gift products.
