PCSO Website Redesign

Type: Academic Project — Website UI Design

Subject Course: Human-Computer Interaction+

Year: 2024

Role/Contributions: UI Designer

Tool used: Canva

General Description

The PCSO Website Redesign project was developed under the Human-Computer Interaction course that aims to improve the structure and usability of the Philippine Charity Sweepstakes Office (PCSO) website. The original website design showed challenges in terms of cluttered layout, and low accessbility, which affects the user’s experience and navigation.

The redesign focuses on creating a more user-frienldy, organized, efficient, and accessible interface. The layout was restructured to highlight important information such as lotto results, charity services, and announcements in a clear and readable format.

Objectives

Redesign the PCSO website to improve clarity, readability, and navigation.

Present important information through a structured and modern interface.

Target Users

Filipino citizens who often check lotto results or PCSO services.

Users seeking assisstence about PCSO programs.

Features

Simplified homepage layout

Organized lotto result display

Clear navigation and caregorization of services.
