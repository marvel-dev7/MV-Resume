General Description

Many individuals struggle to maintain a healthy balance between their nutrition, fitness, and daily tasks due to the lack of an organized system to manage these aspects. The SideKlick application is a lifestyle management mobile application designed to assist users in managing various aspects of their daily lives including their health, tasks, and routines through a comprehensive suite of tools.

The app combines a variety of tools for wellness and productivity, from financial tracking to skincare reminders, into all-in-one cohesive platform. Aiming to provide them an intuitive and friendly interface, it empowers users to take control of their well-being while simplifying daily organization.

Objectives

Promote financial efficiency and spending awareness

Enhance time management through smart scheduling tools

Support Nutritional Goals

Facilitate Fitness Management

Encourage proper hydration and healthy lifestyle habits

Ensure food safety and allergen awareness for better meal planning

Provide skincare management and reminders for daily self-care

Target Users

Primary Users: Individuals interested in managing their health, productivity, and wellness.

Secondary Users: Fitness enthusiasts, nutritionists, and health professionals who manage multiple clients or personal goals.
