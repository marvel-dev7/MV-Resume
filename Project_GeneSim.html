GeneSim (Genetics Simulation)

Type: Academic Project — Desktop App UI Design

Subject Course: Modelling and Simulation

Year: 2023

Role/Contributions: Lead UI designer

Tool used: Qt Designer

General Description

GeneSim is an educational desktop application developed for the Modelling and Simulation course. This innovative application is designed to simulates people's genetic inheritance by predicting possible physical attributes, traits, and potential health issues of a child based on their parental characteristcs. It provides users informative experience of understanding how genetics can influence physical appearance and inherited conditions.

By combining science and technology, GeneSim serves as both an educational and interactive tool as well as, entertainment, or personal curiosity, offering a fascinating insight into the world of genetics. It allows users to visualize genetic outcomes through simulation, making more engaging and accessible for students and general users.

Objectives

To provide an interactive tool for learning basic genetics and inheritance patterns.

To visualize genetic results based on user-defined parental data.

To promote educational engagement through interactive design.

Target Users

Students studying genetics and biology.

Educators seeking a visual aid for teaching heredity.

Individuals curious about genetic simulations and traits inheritance.

Features

Physical Attributes Simulation (e.g., eye color, height, hair type)

Genetic Disease Simulation

Interactive Input for Parental Traits

Visual Result Display

Login Page

Genetic Health Simulator

Physical Feature Simulator

Traits Simulator
